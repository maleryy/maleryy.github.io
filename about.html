I am an aspiring art conservator, Southeast Asianist, papermaker, and bread baker. I am very interested in investigating history through the labor and creativity of people who made things with their hands. I seek to challenge how artisans and craftspeople are defined by their labor capital through investigations of their individual creative authority in history.
From the fibers that make up paper, to the mortar that upholds libraries, I'm especially curious about the study of Southeast Asian print history and its relevance in art historical, musuem, and academic spaces.
