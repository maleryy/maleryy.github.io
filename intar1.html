aggregation, 2018

bristol paper and fishing line, 1x1x1'

Aggregation is based on the production of 2D and 3D objects made of sheet material followed by the exploration of relevant assembly methods permitting the construction of larger entities installed in a framework constructed of metal studs. Concentrated light, scattered or reflected by the objects in space create a complex projected world of shadows. This project investigates the juxtaposition of the physical object with itâ€™s projected image through photographs capturing motion, pattern, and ambience.
