inter-space, 2018

redesigning the RISD Bank Building

This project is an architectural intervention into an existing interior space, the second floor of the RISD Bank Building. The process was rooted in the composition of a narrative, definition of a program, and the finding of form. My program was a collaborative workspace for interdisciplinary studies, following a physical and conceptual narrative reifying the pedagogical chasm between Brown and RISD as educational institutions.

Featured at the 2018 RISD Interior Architecture Triennial Exhibition.
