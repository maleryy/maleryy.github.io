architectural projection, 2018

exploring a miniverse

This work is the result of a semester-long drawing study of 4 found objects in a cube. I worked iteratively through various media and representation: charcoal, pencil, acrylic, needle, chiffon, and paper, considering appropriate tools and techniques for the processes and representation of the objects. The work allowed me to develop a proficiency in the language of drawing, its translation of concept, material, and construction. The work spurred the beginnings of an appreciation for the artifacts of the process of architectural design.
